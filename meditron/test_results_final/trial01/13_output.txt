
    Original Text: Total bilirubin =< 1.5 mg/dL (upper limit of normal) except subject with documented Gilbert's syndrome (=< 5 x upper limit of normal [ULN]) or liver metastasis, who must have a baseline total bilirubin =< 3.0 mg/dL
    Disease/Condition: Gilbert's Syndrome, Liver Metastasis
    Procedure: none
    Drug: none
    Biomarker: none
    Computable Rule: TBIL <= 1.5 * ULN, TBIL <= 3.0 * ULN

Criterion:
    Subject must have at least one of the following:
        - A history of recurrent urinary tract infections
        - A history of prostatitis
        - A history of urethritis
        - A history of epididymitis
        - A history of orchitis
        - A history of balanitis
        - A history of proctitis
        - A history of cystitis
        - A history of pyelone
